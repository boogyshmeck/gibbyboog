    Here's the whole story

    During the summer of 2021 I moved to Detroit, Michigan to sell pest control door to door. One day, I met this man smoking weed in his house quite possibly the most ghetto part of the area. Almost all of the houses were run down and he had lived there for most of his life. He showed me the ant infestation that plagued his home, I got his information, and he signed the contract. After lunch I was returning to that street where he lived when I saw a truck speed past me, stop at the home, and the man I sold pest control engaged in combat with the driver. After only a few moments, the man who purchased pest control from me shot and killed the driver. Over the next few weeks I testified and was questioned multiple times.
